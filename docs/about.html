About Us

Our History

VKellogg’s was founded by Will Keith Kellogg in 1906 in Battle Creek, Michigan. With a passion for creating nutritious and delicious breakfast foods, Will Keith Kellogg pioneered the production of ready-to-eat cereal, revolutionizing the way people start their day. Over the years, VKellogg’s has grown into a multinational company renowned for its innovation in the food industry. Today, we are proud to be one of the leading cereal manufacturers worldwide, offering a diverse range of breakfast foods and snacks that cater to all ages.


Our Mission

At VKellogg’s, our mission is to provide wholesome, high-quality foods that bring joy and nutrition to our customers' lives. We are committed to:

1. Innovating for Better Nutrition: Continuously developing new and improved products that meet the evolving needs of our consumers.
2. Promoting Healthy Lifestyles: Encouraging balanced diets and active living through our products and community initiatives.
3. Sustainability: Implementing sustainable practices in our sourcing, production, and packaging to protect our planet for future generations.
4. Community Engagement: Supporting local communities through charitable contributions, educational programs, and volunteer efforts.

Our Commitment to Quality

Quality is at the heart of everything we do at VKellogg’s. We ensure that all our products are made with the finest ingredients, free from artificial colors and flavors. Our rigorous quality control processes guarantee that every bite you take meets our high standards for taste and nutrition.
